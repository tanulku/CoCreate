THE PROGRAM

As an OMERS member, your relationship with us can last a lifetime. CoCreate is your opportunity to help shape the OMERS member experience and create something from the ground up with a group of cutting edge researchers and innovators.

"The value in being an OMERS member starts the day you join the Plan."

THE EXPERIENCE

Joining the CoCreate community means being part of meaningful and interesting work. You’ll be partnering with our Research and Innovation team to craft exciting new projects – meaning you can have a direct impact on the design of new member products and services while improving all aspects of your overall experience.

You’ll have the opportunity to participate on a volunteer basis or for a chance to win awesome prizes.
